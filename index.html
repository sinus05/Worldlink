FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What English courses do you offer?

We offer General English (Beginner to Advanced), Academic English, Business English, IELTS and exam preparation, Dissertation Support, Business Communication, Virtual Meeting Support, Visa Application Support, Study & Living Abroad guidance, School & College Applications, and Professional Representation.

Are classes in-person or online?

All classes are held in-person at our academy at Shota Rustaveli 13A, Tashkent. We do not currently offer online classes.

Are lessons individual or in a group?

All lessons at WorldLink Academy are individual (one-on-one). Every student receives dedicated, personalised instruction tailored to their specific goals and learning pace.

Is there a free trial lesson?

Yes — we offer a free trial lesson for all new students so you can experience the teaching style before committing. Book your free trial through our enquiry form or call us on +998 77 481 11 22.

How much do courses cost?

Monthly pricing starts from 1,170,000 UZS for one lesson per week (4 lessons/month). The most popular plan is 3 days per week — 12 lessons per month — at 3,500,000 UZS. Full-week intensive study (7 days, 28 lessons/month) is 8,120,000 UZS. Every lesson is 1 hour 30 minutes.

How long does IELTS preparation take?

It depends on your starting level and target band score. Most students benefit from 2–4 months of focused preparation. Book a free consultation and we will assess your current level and recommend a personalised study plan.

Can you help with UK student visa applications?

Yes. Our Visa Application Support service covers document preparation, personal statement guidance, and interview coaching specifically for UK study visa applications.

How do I enrol at WorldLink Academy?

Fill in our enquiry form or call +998 77 481 11 22. We will arrange a free trial lesson and assessment, then recommend the course and schedule that suits you best.
